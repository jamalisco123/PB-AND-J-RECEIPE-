PB&J RECEIPE!

learn how to make a simple peanut butter and sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. spread peanut butter on the slice.
  3. spread jelly om the other slice.
  4. put the two slices together
